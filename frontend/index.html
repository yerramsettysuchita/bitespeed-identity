Algorithm

How reconciliation works

Three decision paths. One outcome: the complete identity cluster of a customer.

1

New Contact

No matching email or phone found. Creates a fresh contact with linkPrecedence="primary" and returns it with empty secondary IDs.

2

Secondary Created

A match is found but the request has new info. A secondary contact is created, linked to the oldest primary via linkedId.

3

Primary Merge

Request links two separate primaries. The newer one is demoted to secondary and all its children re-link to the true primary.
